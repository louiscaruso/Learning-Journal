# Operators and loop notes
- ` == ` is equal to
    - This operator compares two values (numbers, strings, booleans) to see if they are the same
- ` != ` is not equal to 
- ` === ` is strict equal to 
- ` !== ` is strict not equal to
- ` > ` greater than 
- ` < ` less than
- ` >= ` greater than or equal to 
- ` <= ` less than or equal to
